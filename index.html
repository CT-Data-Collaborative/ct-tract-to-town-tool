Connecticut

Census Tract to Town

Converter

This tool lets you convert Connecticut Census tracts into towns where these tracts are located. In the input area, type one or more tract numbers. They can be short (eg 303 for Darien) or long (9007610100 for Clinton or 1400000US09001220200 for New Fairfield). Keep it one per line. Press the arrow button to convert.
